About

Marvel Entertainment, LLC is a media and entertainment company that owns Marvel Comics and Marvel Studios. Marvel is known for its characters, which have appeared in a variety of media over 75 years. Marvel's characters include superheroes like Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Daredevil, Hulk, and Thor, as well as other characters like the Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, and Doctor Strange.
